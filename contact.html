Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Shipping within South Africa typically takes 2-5 business days. International shipping can take 7-14 business days, depending on the destination country and customs processing.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. Items must be unused and in their original packaging. Please note that custom-made items cannot be returned unless there is a manufacturing defect.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. International customers may be responsible for import duties and taxes as required by their country's regulations.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your package on our website or through the carrier's tracking system.
