About NovaNest

Our story, mission, and values

Our Story

NovaNest was founded in 2020 with a simple yet powerful vision: to showcase the incredible craftsmanship of South African artisans to the world.

What began as a small passion project in Cape Town has grown into a thriving online marketplace connecting talented local creators with customers who appreciate authentic, handcrafted goods.

Our founder, Thabo Nkosi, traveled across South Africa discovering hidden talents and traditional crafts that were at risk of being forgotten in our fast-paced modern world. Inspired by the dedication and skill of these artisans, he created NovaNest as a platform to celebrate their work and bring it to a global audience.

Our Mission

At NovaNest, our mission is to:

  • Showcase South African Talent

    We provide a platform for local artisans to share their exceptional craftsmanship with the world.

  • Support Sustainable Practices

    We prioritize environmentally conscious products and ethical production methods.

  • Preserve Cultural Heritage

    We celebrate and help preserve traditional South African crafting techniques and designs.

  • Connect Globally

    We bridge the gap between local artisans and international markets, creating economic opportunities.
